To highlight a specific number in the table, enter it in the "Open Number" field and click Highlight.

3. Updating via Message

You can update multiple numbers and prices at once using the "Update From Message" feature. Use the format:

"25":100.00; "49":50.00; "15":100.00

4. Download PDF

Click Download PDF to save the current table and totals as a PDF file.

5. Logs

The bottom panel shows all your actions and updates. You can clear logs anytime using the Clear Log button.
